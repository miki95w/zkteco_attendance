✅ AUTOMATIC DAILY RECORD GENERATION (6:00 AM)
   • Creates attendance records for all active employees
   • Assigns shifts automatically

✅ REAL-TIME STATUS TRACKING
   🟢 PRESENT      → Employee punched in with complete records
   🔴 ABSENT       → No punch recorded
   🟡 MISSED PUNCH → Forgot to punch out
   🔵 ON LEAVE     → Approved leave

✅ AUTOMATIC ABSENCE MARKING (11:00 PM)
   • Marks employees as absent if no punch recorded
   • 1-day grace period (marks yesterday)
   • Automatic remark added
   • No manual work needed!

Automated Jobs (Cron):

1️⃣  Generate Daily Records
    • Runs: Daily at 6:00 AM
    • Action: Creates records for all employees
    • Status: Active

2️⃣  Auto-Mark Absent
    • Runs: Daily at 11:00 PM
    • Action: Marks yesterday's absences
    • Status: Active

3️⃣  Process Attendance Logs
    • Runs: Every 1 minute
    • Action: Processes device data
    • Status: Active

Access: Settings → Technical → Scheduled Actions
